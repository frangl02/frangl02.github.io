ABOUT.

I'm a 3D Generalist and Visual Designer based in Argentina, working remotely with agencies and brands.

I specialize in creating high-quality 3D visuals for advertising, motion pieces and branded content. My workflow combines strong visual aesthetics with efficiency, allowing me to deliver polished results with fast turnaround times.

I work mainly with Blender and After Effects, handling projects end-to-end — from concept and modeling to lighting, animation, rendering and final compositing.

My focus is on clean design, strong visual impact and clear communication throughout every stage of production.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills toward real-time workflows and gaming-oriented visuals using Unity, focusing on game asset modeling and interactive-ready environments.
